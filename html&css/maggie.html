How to make Maggie

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pack of Maggie noodles
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water in a pan.
  2. Cook the noodles for 2-3 minutes until they are soft and cooked through.
  3. Add the masala which comes with packet
  4. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggie
