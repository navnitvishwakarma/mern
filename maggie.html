How to make Maggie

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pack of Maggie noodles
  2. 2 cups of water
  3. 1 tablespoon of oil
  4. 1/2 cup of chopped vegetables (optional)
  5. Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil the water in a pan.
  2. Add the oil and chopped vegetables (if using) to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the vegetables for 2-3 minutes until they are slightly tender.
  4. Add the Maggie noodles and the tastemaker (seasoning) that comes with the pack.
  5. Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the noodles are soft and cooked through.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggie
